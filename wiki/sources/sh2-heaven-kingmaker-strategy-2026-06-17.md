## Raw Concept

Community **Kingmaker opening guide** (Matt Logan, SH2 Heaven) — concrete build order, popularity/tax balance, estate outsourcing, carter quirks. PvE-focused; not ranked meta.

## Narrative

### Core economy philosophy [CONFIRMED — guide author]

- **Massive excess** — produce far more food than +8 rations consume; sell surplus at market
- **Three gold sources:** tax, market sales, estate taxes (lower rate than primary)
- **Weapon buying** over crafting — author rarely builds weapons; buys with market gold
- **Priority:** wood + food first → castle services → honor buildings

### Opening build order (abbreviated) [CONFIRMED]

| Step | Build |
|------|-------|
| 1–2 | 2–3 adjacent stockpiles |
| 3 | Granary (+1 ration immediately) |
| 4–8 | 5 woodcutters, 5 apple, 5 dairy, 4 wheat, 1 mill, 6 bakers |
| 9–12 | 6 hovels, armory (inner wall), barracks, immediate archers on keep + braziers |
| 13+ | Merc post (50+ horse archers), gong×3, falconer×3, hunters×6, double rations |
| Crime | Courthouse + 5 stocks OR executioner route; guard posts (granary + treasury) |
| Honor | Lord's kitchen, Lady bedchamber, market (sell wool/pitch/hops/ale/iron/grapes) |
| Phase 2 | Joust, fair, church (+6 religion), quarries, stables |

### Popularity automation target [CONFIRMED]

Author's "set and forget" Kingmaker balance:

| Category | Setting |
|----------|---------|
| Church | +6 (Special Mass) |
| Food | +8 (Double rations) |
| Entertainment | +10 (Joust + Fair overlap) |
| Tax | **–16 or –12** (Extra Cruel / Cruel) |

Net: high tax income while popularity stays >50.

### Estates & crime outsourcing [TENTATIVE — author experience]

- Move **recidivist industries** (innkeeper, vintner) to secondary estates
- **10+ carter posts** per productive estate hauling to primary
- Kingmaker → quasi free-build after subjugating AIs

### Known quirks [CONFIRMED — author]

- Stockpile **blocks production** when full; granary **does not** overfill block
- Carters **auto-move** apples/wood/stone/iron/pitch; **luxury goods need manual route**
- Executioner **bugs** — author prefers 5× stocks
- Knights **lose horses** — delete/recruit workaround

### castle-sim steal

- Document **tax+ration+church+entertainment** as preset "automation profile"
- Carter manual vs auto routing = UX feature for estates phase
- Stockpile-full stall = must implement production pause feedback
