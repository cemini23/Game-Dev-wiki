## Raw Concept

Ubisoft **Teammates** — playable research demo (FPS) extending Neo NPC work: voice assistant **Jaspar** + AI squad mates **Sofia/Pablo** with real-time voice commands.

## Narrative

### Mechanics [CONFIRMED]

- Player voice commands: highlight enemies, lore, settings, pause
- Squad NPCs take tactical orders (cover, timing, targets)
- Research goal: natural speech immersion, not shipping product

### Design insight — narrative fences [CONFIRMED — Virginie Mosser]

Writers author story, personality, and **boundaries**; NPCs improvise within fences — not replace scripted canon.

> "Instead of fixed lines, we create fences that let NPCs improvise within the world but stay within lore and motivations."

### Human + AI division [CONFIRMED]

Team explicitly rejects replacing creatives — combine tech strengths with human story direction.

### castle-sim relevance

- Voice commander for units = Tier 3+ fantasy (Stronghold has click orders, not speech)
- **Fences pattern** applies if ever adding LLM advisor dialogue
- Closed playtest feedback loop — model for guardrail iteration
