### Teaching spine (economy/combat)

| Phase | Missions | Systems stressed |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| Micro + rebuild | pow1–2 | Lord horse combat; quarry/ox/bridge; cloth chain |
| First siege | pow3 | Ladder assault; Ctrl+0–9 groups |
| Multi-quest map | pow4 | Side quests + border defense |
| Monastery arc | pow5 | Full economy reset; military wipe |
| Estates war | pow6 | **6-estate control**; multi-enemy |
| Abbey defense | pow7 | Honour buy estate; ale buy vs brew; tax/rations |
| Hawk arc | pow8 | Rescue lord; **multi-lord elimination**; tunnels (pow8-2) |
| Betrayal ladder | pow9–12 | Ranked endgame; kill King win |

### castle-sim notes

- PoW assumes **carry-over saves** less than PoP but still sequential difficulty ramp
- pow6-1 is first **estate-count win condition** — maps to Kingmaker estate race
- pow12 = **King kill only** — no honour victory; pure siege finale
