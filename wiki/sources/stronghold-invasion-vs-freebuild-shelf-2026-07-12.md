## Raw Concept

Digest pick (2026-07-12): **Northern Strategist** essay — SH1 **Invasion Mode** (scripted time pressure + atmosphere shift) never got a dedicated successor; later titles leaned **Free Build** and skirmish, losing "will your castle survive?" uncertainty.

## Narrative

### Design takeaways [CONFIRMED — video thesis]

| Theme | Invasion | Free build |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Pressure | External timer + surprise waves | Player-triggered invasions |
| Fantasy | Dungeon-Keeper-style survival in one castle | Sandbox architect |
| Tension | Scribe warning → music shift → lord taunt | Optional stress |
| Community | Thousands of custom invasion maps (Helms Deep, gauntlets) | Parallel but different UX |

**castle-sim implication:** SH2-baseline clone should keep **optional sandbox** (@concepts/stronghold-franchise-best-of-shelf.md) but **not** treat free build as the core loop — campaign/kingmaker + timed siege pressure remain north star. SH4 demo mission 2 cited as partial invasion return [TENTATIVE].

**Verdict:** **Steal (design framing)** — scope guardrail, not a new mechanic brief.
