## Raw Concept

Pre-release **GameSpy** interview (Dec 2004) with **Simon Bradbury** on Stronghold 2 — fan-driven changes, 2D→3D gameplay impact, castle building UX.

## Narrative

### Top fan request: no sword-wall hacking [CONFIRMED]

SH1 allowed foot soldiers to chop walls — expedient for balance under time pressure. SH2 shifts to **siege equipment breaches**, wall scaling, fighting on/along walls. Directly informs castle-sim Tier 2 siege design; v0 can defer breach mechanics.

### 3D engine gameplay wins [CONFIRMED]

- Interior tower combat (spiral stairs) — animation depth impossible in 2D frame limits
- **Architect view** — Spacebar top-down for wall planning; spots gaps easily
- Thicker walls, "paint" deep walls without 2D gap bugs
- Richer disease/theatrical death animations tied to sim systems

### Castle building UX lesson

3D + architect camera = primary tool for **defensibility review**. castle-sim Tier 1: consider top-down build mode even in 2D isometric (@concepts/art-pipeline-v0-requirements.md).

### Siege equipment tease

Fire/pitch ditches return; new burn traps — details withheld pre-ship.
