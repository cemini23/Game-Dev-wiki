## Narrative

### Founding [CONFIRMED]

- **1999** — Simon Bradbury + Eric Ouellette; ex-Impressions (Lords of the Realm, Caesar)
- Goal: merge **city builder + RTS** without sacrificing either — Stronghold (2001)

### Franchise beats

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2001 | Stronghold — surprise hit (NA, Germany) |
| 2002 | Stronghold: Crusader — Middle East, faster RTS economy |
| 2010–2017 | Stronghold Kingdoms MMO — long F2P tail on Steam |
| 2021 | Acquired by Devolver Digital |
| 2023 | Stronghold: Definitive Edition — return to isometric 2D roots; 100k sales in 72h |
| 2025 | Crusader: Definitive Edition — 250k units in 3 weeks |

### Scale today [CONFIRMED]

~40 staff; 13M+ lifetime Stronghold sales; BAFTA nominated.

### Hobby project implications

- **Definitive Edition success** validates fan appetite for **SH1/Crusader feel** — aligns with castle-sim Tier 1 scope (@concepts/scope-tiers.md)
- New UE Stronghold in development — wiki tracks SH1/Crusader systems for Tier 1, not unreleased UE feature set
