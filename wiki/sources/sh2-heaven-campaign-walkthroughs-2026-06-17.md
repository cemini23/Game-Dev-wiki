### Path of War — sampled economy beats [CONFIRMED — walkthroughs]

| Chapter | Teaching focus |
|---------|----------------|
| 1 | Lord combat tutorial — decoy spearmen |
| 2-1 | Stockpile/granary/quarry/ox — bridge repair |
| 2-2 | Sheep → cloth chain; hunters for food |
| 2-3 | Market sell cloth → spearmen; wall defense vs berserkers |
| 3 | First **siege** — ladders, archer groups, formation shortcuts |
| 6 | Multi-estate **rats/gong** + carter food aid + falconer |
| 7 | Honour/tax/ration balance; buy estate (300 honour); ale purchase vs brew |
| 11-1 | Late-game full economy + **estate capture** |
| 12 | Final siege — kill King before rivals |

### Recurring walkthrough tactics [CONFIRMED]

- **Granary adjacency** to farms (pop1, pop2)
- **Pause** at mission start for layout (pop6, pow7)
- **Carter posts** for multi-estate food/stone (pop3, pop6)
- **Hunters** over melee vs bears (pow2-3)
- **Market weapon/cloth selling** when treasury absent (pow2-2, pow2-3)
- Secondary estate **+10 gold/month** (pop3)

### castle-sim mapping

Use PoP chapters 1–6 as **single-player tutorial spine** before Kingmaker sandbox — mirrors honour/popularity/crime/estates unlock order in @concepts/stronghold-2-campaign-economy-curriculum.md.
