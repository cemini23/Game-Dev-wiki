## Raw Concept

Exclusive pre-release **GameSpot** interview (Nov 3, 2004) with Simon Bradbury — complements @sources/gamespy-stronghold-2-bradbury-qa-2004.md with **honor/lord-sim**, religion/ale UX redesign, campaign modes, and interior keep combat.

## Narrative

### Design pillars [CONFIRMED — Bradbury]

- **50% combat / 50% castle management** — more breathing room vs SH1's combat-heavy pacing
- **~50% more economic chains** — sheep/weaving, royal foods, falconry, honour
- **"Living like a lord"** — feasts, jousts, holy days, marriage, fairs, trials — highly visual
- Fan feedback: **no wall hacking**, formations, architect view, fallback points

### Honour as primary resource [CONFIRMED]

Generated by **lord sim** activities; spent on units, estates, rank promotions. Bradbury: *"new primary resource, honor, and all the interesting ways to expend this resource."*

### Religion & ale — visibility fix [CONFIRMED]

SH1 systems were "too technical and hidden." SH2 makes them **visual events**:

- **Church:** long service interrupts duties → large **popularity** bonus at end (visual)
- **Ale:** drink at inn → on-screen popularity pop; peasant staggers away **unable to work**

castle-sim clone: monthly tick needs **event feedback**, not silent stat changes.

### Modes & setting [CONFIRMED]

- European setting; post-Viking era; **Olaf Grimtooth** + eight intertwined characters
- **Path of War** vs **Path of Peace** (calmer, pageantry-focused campaign)
- Sandbox, historical siege scenarios, skirmish (7 AI), **8-player multiplayer**
- New skirmish extension teased (later revealed as **Kingmaker**)

### 3D gameplay wins [CONFIRMED]

- Smooth scroll/zoom/rotate; solid castle feel
- **Open virtually every building** — fight inside keep (4 levels), tower stair fights
- Physics: smoke, fire, wall explosions, units thrown off walls
- Routing units through multi-floor keeps = hard technical problem; unique selling point

### Siege mechanics delta [CONFIRMED]

1. **Breach** with siege engines — defending engineers **repair** unless attackers hold breach
2. **Ladders + siege towers** — fight along walls; inner-wall crossbow vs pinned attackers
3. **>2× unit types** (interview cut off)

### castle-sim implications

| SH2 feature | Clone priority |
|-----------|----------------|
| Visual church/ale events | Phase C UX |
| Keep interior fights | Phase E optional |
| Engineer wall repair | Phase D siege |
| Honour spend gates | Phase B–D spine |
