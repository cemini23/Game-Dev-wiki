## Raw Concept

MobyGames entry for **FireFly Studios' Stronghold 2** (Moby ID 17825) — release metadata, Deluxe additions, critic/player summaries.

## Narrative

### Release

- **Ship:** 2005-04-18 Windows (2K Games)
- **Engine middleware:** Granny 3D, Bink, Miles AIL, LIFESTUDIO:HEAD [CONFIRMED — MobyGames credits groups]
- **Steam Edition:** 2017 — Workshop, achievements, MP

### Feature summary (official description)

3D castle builder + RTS; dual campaign (peace/economy vs military); crime/punishment; honour from feasts/jousting; waste/pest; expanded traps; Kingmaker; historical sieges.

### Deluxe edition additions

- Patch 1.3.1 integrated; extra AI: Queen, Pontifex, Sir Grey
- Maps: Korea, Europe; scenery To the Sea, Three Bridges
- Historical castles: Windsor, Edinburgh, Haut-Koenigsburg, Tower of London, Fougeres, Harlech, Heuneburg

### Design caution (player review synthesis)

Economy vs invasion pacing can feel disjointed; troop formation micro tedious — useful QoL targets for personal clone.

## Snippets

Series position: after Castle Attack 2 (2004), before Stronghold Legends (2006).
