## Raw Concept

Practical framework: adaptive NPCs as **systems** requiring control, memory design, guardrails, regression testing, and LiveOps cost caps.

## Narrative

### Key sections [CONFIRMED]

1. Failure modes: unpredictability, exploits, tone drift
2. Control: narrow goals, explicit constraints, authorial intent
3. Memory: curated state, decay, no courtroom permanence
4. Guardrails: safety, lore checks, economy bands
5. Testing: seeds, replay, behavior snapshots
6. LiveOps: inference cost as per-DAU tax; seasonal behavior sets vs endless learning
7. Metrics: session depth, quest completion stability — not "players noticed AI"

### Quote

> "Agentic NPCs are not about giving characters freedom. They are about earning player confidence through consistency, fairness, and believable constraints."
