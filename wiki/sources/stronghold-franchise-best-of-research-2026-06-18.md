## Raw Concept

Cross-franchise feature harvest for **castle-sim** — operator baseline **Stronghold 2**, target **best-of-series** feel without fantasy bloat or mercenary economy break.

## Narrative

### Method

Brave search + existing wiki (SH1 manual, Bradbury SH3 interview, SH2 Heaven ingest, Fandom/Wikipedia). No new retail play — synthesis for `@concepts/stronghold-franchise-best-of-shelf.md`.

### Per-title notes [TENTATIVE unless cited elsewhere]

| Title | Standout features | Community / designer signal |
|-------|-------------------|----------------------------|
| **SH1 / DE** | Fear factor (good/bad buildings → efficiency ↔ pop ↔ troop damage); moat, pitch, killing pits; siege-only scenarios; real-time map editor; unit stances/patrol/fortify | DE remaster QoL; **no skirmish AI** in SH1 DE (Crusader invented builder-AI) |
| **Crusader / DE** | 8 skirmish AI lords; **lord binks** (video taunts); Skirmish Trail; custom skirmish alliances; mercenary post; assassin, horse archer, fire ballista; oasis map scarcity | Forum consensus: **best skirmish AI** in series; mercs criticized for breaking workshop fantasy |
| **SH2** | Honour, crime, estates, kingmaker, 3D architect, lord activities | Operator baseline; AI less moddable than Crusader; no lord binks |
| **Legends** | **Player picks estate production** on capture; King of the Hill; streamlined economy | Fantasy units (dragons, giants) — cult hit but off-theme for realistic clone |
| **SH3** | Free-angle walls; night fog-of-war sieges; hovel quality ∝ keep distance; diseased-animal siege ammo; Havok crumble | Bradbury: return to SH1; **removed crime**; **no Kingmaker/skirmish** at launch — cautionary |
| **Crusader II** | Lord binks return; co-op skirmish; housing-from-keep (SH3); sandbox builder | SH3 engine; refined desert skirmish |
| **Warlords** | Capturable **warlords** + diplomacy edicts; clothing/tea chains; **6 housing tiers**; aesthetics ↔ population; hero unit abilities (Military Academy) | Warlords = estate system successor; fear factor + cosmetics return |
| **Crusader DE (2025)** | Co-op skirmish trail; 4 new AI lords; Bedouin units; larger maps; up to 20 lords | Fan-requested QoL bundle — steal **social/co-op patterns**, not 2D engine |

### Firefly meta-lessons [CONFIRMED — Bradbury SH3 interview]

- SH2 **overreach** vs SH1 simplicity — but operator explicitly wants SH2 identity
- Large maps rejected — extra march time without combat
- Event-driven night beats > continuous day/night sim
- More units ≠ better

## Snippets

Wikipedia: Crusader lord binks absent in SH2, returned in Crusader II.

Warlords: warlords are "successors of the estate system" with edicts and capture mechanics.

Legends Fandom: estates allow **player-chosen goods type** on capture vs map-fixed SH2.
